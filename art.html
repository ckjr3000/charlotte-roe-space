Artist Bio

Charlotte Roe is an interdisciplinary digital artist based in Huddersfield, United Kingdom. Stemming from a curiosity and love of technology, she makes work with and ‘in collaboration with’ computers exploring the human relationships we cultivate with technology. Roe has previously worked with sculpture, illustration, painting, sound and programming to explore these themes.

She has exhibited work at Huddersfield Contemporary Music Festival (2020 and 2021) and Roam Festival (2021). Her work has been featured on BBC Radio 3’s New Music Show and BBC Radio 4’s Pick Of The Week.
