Teaching

Let’s make comics with onomatopoeia!

November 2021, Workshop co-designed and delivered with Ryoko Akama. Comissioned by Outlands Network. Introducing participants to Japanese onomatopoeic words & concepts in comic illustration, helping facilitate the creation of comic strips from narratives created with onomatapoeic words.

Song In a Day.

June 2021. Co-delivered with Frazer Merrick. Day workshop with a LGBTQ Youth Group teaching online music making tools and song writing techniques with the goals of each participant producing a ‘song in a day’.

Sculpting Sound.

March 2020, Workshop designed and delivered for CLIP Sound and Music. Aimed at 11 – 16 year old musicians, inviting them to consider sound as a sculptural material.
